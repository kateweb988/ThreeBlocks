CryptoCubes is a game
based on four principles

01/ Simplicity

CryptoCubes has simple rules that any player will understand. Behind this simplicity lies an infinite number of combinations and strategies for winning. They are yours. It is enough to press Play and prove that you are a true Cubeast!

We were inspired both by classic games like tic-tac-toe and sea battle and by such “gambling” things as “Traffic Light24”. This project has long ceased to exist (and its reputation was, to put it mildly, not the most pleasant), but we thought -

Hey, but we can do better, easier and more interesting!

Sometimes bad examples lead to good concepts. Then the idea of CryptoCubes was born, and now we can proudly say that our project is a damn good game cubed.

02/ Yield

We do not do “something out there gambling”. In our game, there is no cunning casino that manages bets, just as there are no bets themselves: players compete with each other, showing their analytical skills, the ability to think a few steps ahead and just know what the wisdom of the crowd is.

However, cubists earn real money with us! No luck - just skill. And therein lies the key to profitability: the casino does not guarantee a loss, because this is...

А game played by real people. Time to start the round!

PLAY
03/ Safety

Funds are not stored on the internal services of the game, so it is impossible to hack the base, find players or illegally withdraw funds. All other users see is the TronLink wallet address.

CryptoCubes are a comfort zone with simple geometry. We are not trying to reinvent the wheel, but create a safe environment for community members to simply play and simply win by withdrawing their income in two clicks. Isn't that how the real game should be?

04/ Communication

CryptoCubes is the "Mafia" in the world of simple games. Subtle psychological manipulation, a simple chat message, and you can be the winner. From how you behave, how you “feel” the mood of other players - victory or loss depends.

We aim not just to drive people into the game, but to turn this process into an exciting near-game experience in which you can find rivals, friends, and maybe even … Love?

We are glad to meet you!

CryptoCubes are four edges that fold into a square and then, scaling, into a cube. Our team is confident that this cube will become a hypercube and will be able to penetrate into other dimensions of interaction between people and technology.

We are happy to work on the project and want our users to
know us and our passion.
