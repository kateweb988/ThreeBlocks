How To Play

You play against other players under simple game conditions that add excitement to your enjoyment.

Choose the Crypto Cube that will have the fewest players.

  • The Game lasts from 15 minutes
  • Choose a Crypto Cube to participate in the game
  • If there are many players on your crypto cube, choose another one to win.
  • You can switch between crypto cubes any number of times
  • A few minutes before the end of the game, new players are not allowed
  • Winners take the entire pool of the bank from other crypto cubes
  • The crypto cube with the fewest players at the end of the game wins.
